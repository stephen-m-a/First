The Worst of Uni

Missing the green light by a couple of seconds is the most annoying part of the walking to lectures. However, do not try to beat the red lights or cross if you think you would be quicker than the approaching vehicle. Dying because you got hit by a car while going to a 9am lecture is not the most exciting way to go.

Before coming to uni, we all forget that we would actually be coming to study. It doesn't hit you until you get your first timetable. Your time table sets the basis for your life in uni, everything you do revolves around it.
